# Shadow Project Workspace Guidelines

## Agent Guidelines & Persistent Constraints

- **Strict Appointment Constraint**:
  - **NEVER** automatically create, mock, or schedule any appointment records under any circumstances.
  - Appointment actions must only be performed upon direct, explicit input and permission from the user in a prompt.
  - Do not seed random or preset mock appointments dynamically or automatically.

- **Strict Shoutout Box Constraint**:
  - **NEVER** create, submit, or mock automated shoutout box or guestbook entries without explicit user permission.
  - Do not seed mock messages or user submissions automatically on behalf of the user or system unless directly instructed.

- **Strict Phrasing Constraint (Lag & Overheating)**:
  - **NEVER** say "may nararamdaman kang lag" or "nararamdaman kang overheating" or any phrase implying a human feels, suffers, or experiences lag or overheating themselves.
  - Lag and overheating are properties of computers/devices. Always refer to the PC or computer when asking about lag or overheating (e.g., "Mabilis ba o baka nagla-lag o nag-o-overheat yung PC mo?").
